# Psiquiatria Aberta BR
An open Brazilian repository for practical psychiatry education in Portuguese.

## About
Psiquiatria Aberta BR is an open knowledge project focused on mental health education and practical psychiatric resources for clinicians, residents, medical students, and the broader community in Brazil.
The goal is to improve access to accurate, concise, and clinically useful mental health information in Portuguese. The repository brings together educational summaries, practical guides, and reference materials that support both learning and real-world clinical reasoning.

## Why this project exists
There is still a gap in Portuguese-language mental health resources that are both accessible and clinically oriented. Many available materials are either too superficial for healthcare professionals or too technical for broader educational use.
This project aims to bridge that gap by creating open, structured, and practical content that can be useful for:
- Psychiatry residents
- Medical students
- General practitioners
- Healthcare professionals
- People interested in mental health education

## Current scope
The repository currently focuses on:
- Educational summaries of major psychiatric topics
- Practical guides for clinical reasoning
- Mental health literacy content in Portuguese
- Curated references for further study

## Planned topics
- Anxiety disorders
- Depressive disorders
- Psychotic disorders
- Sleep disorders
- Psychiatric emergencies
- Psychopharmacology basics
- Communication in mental health care
