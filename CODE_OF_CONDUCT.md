# Code of Conduct
This project is committed to providing a respectful, inclusive, and professional environment for all contributors.

## Our standards
Examples of positive behavior include:
- Being respectful and constructive
- Welcoming feedback
- Focusing on educational value
- Using clear and professional communication

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Misleading medical claims
- Sharing private or identifiable patient information

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with these standards.
